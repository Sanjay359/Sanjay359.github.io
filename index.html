Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 100g Ground Beef
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 Garlic Cloves
  • 400g Canned Tomatoes
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  2. In a pan, cook the ground beef until browned.
  3. Add chopped onion and garlic, and cook until softened.
  4. Add the canned tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Serve the sauce over the spaghetti.

Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  • 500g Chicken Breast
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 Garlic Cloves
  • 1 tbsp Curry Powder
  • 400ml Coconut Milk
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces.
  2. In a pan, cook the onion and garlic until softened.
  3. Add the chicken and cook until browned.
  4. Add the curry powder and cook for another minute.
  5. Pour in the coconut milk, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  6. Serve with rice.

Vegetable Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 Bell Pepper
  • 1 Carrot
  • 1 Broccoli
  • 2 Garlic Cloves
  • 2 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil

Instructions:

  1. Chop all the vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
  2. In a wok, heat the olive oil and cook the garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add the vegetables and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Add the soy sauce and cook for another 2 minutes.
  5. Serve hot.
